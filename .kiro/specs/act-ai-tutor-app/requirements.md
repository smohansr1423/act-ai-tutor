# Requirements Document

## Introduction

An AI-powered ACT test preparation mobile application that provides adaptive learning, original question generation, AI tutoring chat, full test simulation, analytics tracking, and a parent dashboard. The app uses an LLM-based AI layer to generate ACT-style questions, adapt difficulty based on student performance, and deliver personalized study plans across all four ACT sections (English, Math, Reading, Science).

## Glossary

- **App**: The ACT AI Tutoring mobile application running on iOS and Android via Flutter
- **Student**: A registered user who takes practice questions, full tests, and interacts with the AI Tutor
- **Parent**: A registered user who monitors a linked Student's progress via the Parent Dashboard
- **AI_Engine**: The backend AI service responsible for question generation, adaptive recommendations, tutoring chat, and analytics
- **Question_Generator**: The subsystem of AI_Engine that produces original ACT-style questions
- **Adaptive_Engine**: The subsystem of AI_Engine that tracks performance and adjusts difficulty
- **Tutor_Chat**: The conversational AI interface that provides explanations, hints, and concept lessons
- **Analytics_Engine**: The subsystem that computes score trends, accuracy, timing, and weakness data
- **Question_Bank**: The database store of generated and curated questions
- **Section**: One of the four ACT test areas: English, Math, Reading, or Science
- **Skill_Tag**: A label identifying the specific skill or concept tested by a question, drawn from a predefined set per Section
- **Difficulty_Level**: A classification of question complexity as Easy, Medium, or Hard
- **Practice_Mode**: A session where the Student answers questions from a selected Section or mixed topics
- **Full_Test_Mode**: A session simulating a complete ACT section with standard question counts and timing
- **Pacing_Issue**: A detected pattern where the Student's response time exceeds 2 times the median response time for that question's difficulty level
- **Careless_Mistake**: An incorrect answer where the Student previously demonstrated competence on that Skill_Tag
- **Concept_Gap**: An incorrect answer indicating misunderstanding of the underlying concept
- **Performance_Record**: A stored entry linking a Student's response to a question with correctness, time taken, and timestamp
- **Weakness_Profile**: An aggregated view of a Student's accuracy and attempt count per Skill_Tag, calculated over the most recent 20 attempts
- **Study_Plan**: A personalized set of daily practice targets, weekly goals, and score predictions generated by the Adaptive_Engine

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: User Registration and Authentication

**User Story:** As a Student or Parent, I want to create an account and log in securely, so that my progress is saved and my data is protected.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a new user submits valid registration details (name, email, password, role), THE App SHALL validate that the name is between 1 and 100 characters, the email conforms to standard email format, and the password is at least 8 characters containing at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one digit, and SHALL create a user account storing user_id, name, email, grade, and target_score in the Users table
2. IF a new user submits a registration request with an email that already exists in the Users table, THEN THE App SHALL reject the registration and display an error message indicating the email is already in use
3. WHEN a user submits valid login credentials, THE App SHALL authenticate the user and establish a session within 2 seconds
4. IF a user submits invalid login credentials, THEN THE App SHALL display an error message indicating invalid credentials without revealing which field is incorrect
5. IF a user submits invalid login credentials 5 consecutive times, THEN THE App SHALL lock the account for 15 minutes and display a message indicating the account is temporarily locked
6. WHEN a Parent registers, THE App SHALL allow the Parent to link to one or more Student accounts by email invitation
7. THE App SHALL store passwords using a one-way cryptographic hash with a unique salt per user

### Requirement 2: ACT Question Generation

**User Story:** As a Student, I want access to original ACT-style questions across all sections, so that I can practice with realistic content without copyright concerns.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Student requests a practice question for a specified Section and Difficulty_Level, THE Question_Generator SHALL produce an original question containing: question text, four answer choices, correct answer, step-by-step explanation, reasoning for why each incorrect option is wrong, a Skill_Tag, and a strategy tip within 8 seconds
2. THE Question_Generator SHALL produce English questions containing a passage of 2-4 sentences with an underlined portion and four answer choices offering grammatical or stylistic alternatives
3. THE Question_Generator SHALL produce Math questions containing a problem statement with four numerical or expression-based answer choices covering the ACT math content areas (pre-algebra, algebra, geometry, trigonometry)
4. THE Question_Generator SHALL produce Reading questions containing a passage of 200-400 words followed by a comprehension question with four answer choices
5. THE Question_Generator SHALL produce Science questions containing a data representation (described table, graph, or experimental setup) followed by an interpretation question with four answer choices
6. THE Question_Generator SHALL assign a Difficulty_Level (Easy, Medium, or Hard) to each generated question
7. THE Question_Generator SHALL assign exactly one Skill_Tag from a predefined set per Section to each generated question identifying the concept being tested
8. THE Question_Generator SHALL produce only original content and SHALL NOT reproduce copyrighted ACT test material
9. WHEN a question is generated, THE Question_Generator SHALL store it in the Question_Bank with question_id, section, question_text, options, correct_answer, explanation, skill_tag, and difficulty
10. IF the Question_Generator fails to produce a valid question within 8 seconds, THEN THE App SHALL display an error message to the Student and offer the option to retry or select a different Section

### Requirement 3: Practice Mode

**User Story:** As a Student, I want to practice questions by section or in mixed mode, so that I can focus on specific areas or simulate varied conditions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Student selects Practice_Mode, THE App SHALL present section options: English, Math, Reading, Science, or Mixed Mode
2. WHEN the Student selects a Section in Practice_Mode, THE App SHALL present questions from the selected Section one at a time, continuing until the Student ends the session
3. WHEN the Student selects Mixed Mode, THE App SHALL present questions from all four Sections in randomized order
4. WHILE the Student is answering a question, THE App SHALL display a per-question timer showing elapsed time in seconds
5. WHILE the Student is answering a question, THE App SHALL display a "Hint" button in enabled state and an "Explain this" button in disabled state
6. WHEN the Student taps the "Hint" button, THE Tutor_Chat SHALL provide a hint guiding the Student toward the solution without revealing the answer within 5 seconds
7. WHEN the Student submits an answer, THE App SHALL enable the "Explain this" button, and WHEN the Student taps the "Explain this" button, THE App SHALL display the full step-by-step explanation and reasoning for each option
8. WHEN the Student submits an answer, THE App SHALL record a Performance_Record containing user_id, question_id, is_correct, time_taken, and timestamp
9. WHEN the Student taps the "End Session" control during Practice_Mode, THE App SHALL stop presenting new questions, save all Performance_Records, and display a session summary showing total questions answered, number correct, and average time per question

### Requirement 4: Full Test Mode

**User Story:** As a Student, I want to take a full simulated ACT section under timed conditions, so that I can build test-day stamina and assess my readiness.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Student starts Full_Test_Mode for English, THE App SHALL present 75 questions with a 45-minute timer
2. WHEN the Student starts Full_Test_Mode for Math, THE App SHALL present 60 questions with a 60-minute timer
3. WHEN the Student starts Full_Test_Mode for Reading, THE App SHALL present 40 questions with a 35-minute timer
4. WHEN the Student starts Full_Test_Mode for Science, THE App SHALL present 40 questions with a 35-minute timer
5. WHILE the Student is in Full_Test_Mode, THE App SHALL display a countdown timer in minutes and seconds (MM:SS) showing remaining time for the section, updated every second
6. WHEN the section timer expires, THE App SHALL automatically submit all answered questions, mark unanswered questions as skipped, and navigate the Student to the score summary screen within 2 seconds
7. WHEN the Student completes a Full_Test_Mode session, THE App SHALL display a score summary showing the number of correct answers out of total questions, a list of all questions with the Student's selected answer, the correct answer, and an explanation for each question
8. WHILE the Student is in Full_Test_Mode, THE App SHALL allow the Student to navigate forward and backward between questions and to submit the section before the timer expires
9. IF the Student exits or loses connectivity during Full_Test_Mode, THEN THE App SHALL preserve all answers recorded up to that point and allow the Student to resume the session from where they left off within 24 hours
10. IF the Student does not resume an interrupted Full_Test_Mode session within 24 hours, THEN THE App SHALL mark the session as incomplete and discard it without generating a score summary

### Requirement 5: Adaptive Learning

**User Story:** As a Student, I want questions to adjust to my skill level automatically, so that I am always challenged appropriately and my weak areas receive focused attention.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adaptive_Engine SHALL maintain a Weakness_Profile for each Student tracking accuracy and attempt count per Skill_Tag, where accuracy is calculated over the most recent 20 attempts for that Skill_Tag
2. WHEN a Student completes a question, THE Adaptive_Engine SHALL update the Student's Weakness_Profile for the corresponding Skill_Tag by recording whether the attempt was correct and recalculating accuracy over the most recent 20 attempts
3. WHEN the Adaptive_Engine classifies a Student error, THE Adaptive_Engine SHALL categorize it as one of: Concept_Gap (incorrect answer indicating misunderstanding of the underlying concept), Careless_Mistake (incorrect answer where the Student previously demonstrated competence on that Skill_Tag), or Pacing_Issue (correct or incorrect answer where the response time exceeds 2 times the median response time for that question's difficulty level)
4. WHILE a Student has completed at least 5 attempts for a Skill_Tag AND accuracy for that Skill_Tag is below 60%, THE Adaptive_Engine SHALL select Easy questions for that Skill_Tag and provide a concept explanation before each question
5. WHILE a Student has completed at least 5 attempts for a Skill_Tag AND accuracy for that Skill_Tag is between 60% and 80%, THE Adaptive_Engine SHALL select Medium difficulty questions with mixed topics and include timed drills with a time limit of 90 seconds per question
6. WHILE a Student has completed at least 5 attempts for a Skill_Tag AND accuracy for that Skill_Tag is above 80%, THE Adaptive_Engine SHALL select Hard questions with a reduced time limit of 60 seconds per question
7. WHEN the Adaptive_Engine detects a Pacing_Issue for a Student, THE Adaptive_Engine SHALL recommend a timed practice set of 5 to 10 questions with progressively shorter time limits starting at 120 seconds and decreasing by 10 seconds per question
8. WHEN the Student requests a personalized Study_Plan, THE Adaptive_Engine SHALL generate a plan containing between 3 and 10 daily practice targets per weak Skill_Tag, weekly goals with measurable accuracy thresholds, and a projected score range based on current performance data
9. IF a Student has completed fewer than 5 attempts for a Skill_Tag, THEN THE Adaptive_Engine SHALL select Medium difficulty questions for that Skill_Tag until sufficient data is available to determine accuracy

### Requirement 6: AI Tutor Chat

**User Story:** As a Student, I want to ask the AI Tutor questions and get step-by-step help, so that I can understand concepts I find difficult.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Student sends a text message of up to 1000 characters in Tutor_Chat, THE Tutor_Chat SHALL respond with a step-by-step explanation using grade-appropriate vocabulary within 5 seconds
2. WHEN the Student uploads a question image (JPEG, PNG, or GIF, maximum 10 MB) in Tutor_Chat, THE Tutor_Chat SHALL extract the question content and provide a step-by-step explanation within 10 seconds
3. IF the Tutor_Chat fails to extract content from an uploaded image, THEN THE Tutor_Chat SHALL display an error message indicating the image could not be processed and prompt the Student to upload a clearer image or type the question as text
4. WHEN the Student asks for help on a concept, THE Tutor_Chat SHALL provide a hint first, and WHEN the Student requests further help on the same concept, THE Tutor_Chat SHALL reveal the full step-by-step solution
5. THE Tutor_Chat SHALL use motivational and encouraging language in all responses
6. THE Tutor_Chat SHALL explain concepts using grade-appropriate vocabulary based on the Student's registered grade level
7. WHILE the Student is in a Tutor_Chat session, THE Tutor_Chat SHALL maintain conversation context for up to 50 messages so that follow-up questions reference previous exchanges
8. IF the Student sends a text message exceeding 1000 characters, THEN THE Tutor_Chat SHALL reject the message and display an error message indicating the maximum allowed length

### Requirement 7: Analytics Dashboard

**User Story:** As a Student, I want to see my performance trends and weak areas, so that I can understand my progress and focus my study time effectively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Student opens the Analytics Dashboard, THE Analytics_Engine SHALL display a score trend chart showing accuracy percentage over the most recent 30 days per Section, with data points plotted for each day a session was completed
2. WHEN the Student opens the Analytics Dashboard, THE Analytics_Engine SHALL display a list of up to 10 Skill_Tags with accuracy below 60%, ranked from lowest to highest accuracy, each showing the accuracy percentage and attempt count
3. WHEN the Student opens the Analytics Dashboard, THE Analytics_Engine SHALL display average time per question in seconds, broken down by Section, calculated from all Performance_Records within the most recent 30 days
4. WHEN the Student opens the Analytics Dashboard, THE Analytics_Engine SHALL display accuracy percentage per Section calculated from all Performance_Records within the most recent 30 days
5. WHEN a practice or test session is completed, THE Analytics_Engine SHALL update all dashboard metrics within 10 seconds
6. IF the Student opens the Analytics Dashboard and has fewer than 5 Performance_Records for a given Section, THEN THE Analytics_Engine SHALL display a message indicating insufficient data for that Section instead of computed metrics
7. IF the Student opens the Analytics Dashboard and has no Skill_Tags with accuracy below 60%, THEN THE Analytics_Engine SHALL display a message indicating no weak areas identified

### Requirement 8: Parent Dashboard

**User Story:** As a Parent, I want to view my child's study progress and performance, so that I can support their preparation and identify areas needing attention.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Parent with one linked Student opens the Parent Dashboard, THE App SHALL display that Student's overall progress summary including total time spent studying, total sessions completed, and overall accuracy as a percentage across all Sections
2. WHEN a Parent with multiple linked Students opens the Parent Dashboard, THE App SHALL display a list of linked Students and allow the Parent to select one Student whose data is then shown
3. WHEN a Parent opens the Parent Dashboard, THE App SHALL display the linked Student's accuracy trend per Section over the past 30 days as a chart
4. WHEN a Parent opens the Parent Dashboard, THE App SHALL display the linked Student's Skill_Tags with accuracy below 60%, ranked from lowest to highest accuracy, showing a maximum of 10 Skill_Tags
5. THE App SHALL restrict the Parent Dashboard to show only data for Students who have accepted the Parent's link invitation
6. THE App SHALL NOT allow a Parent to modify the Student's study settings or answer questions on the Student's behalf
7. IF a Parent opens the Parent Dashboard and has no linked Students who have accepted an invitation, THEN THE App SHALL display a message indicating no linked Students are available and prompt the Parent to send a link invitation
8. IF a Parent selects a linked Student who has no Performance_Records, THEN THE App SHALL display a message indicating no study data is available yet

### Requirement 9: Question Presentation and Interaction

**User Story:** As a Student, I want a clear and focused question interface, so that I can concentrate on answering without distraction.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE App SHALL display each question with the question text, four labeled answer choices (A, B, C, D), and a visible timer showing elapsed time in Practice_Mode or remaining time in Full_Test_Mode
2. WHEN the Student selects an answer choice, THE App SHALL visually highlight the selected option before submission and allow the Student to change their selection before submitting
3. WHEN the Student submits an answer in Practice_Mode, THE App SHALL indicate whether the answer is correct or incorrect within 1 second
4. WHEN the Student submits an incorrect answer in Practice_Mode, THE App SHALL display the correct answer highlighted along with the explanation
5. WHEN the Student submits a correct answer in Practice_Mode, THE App SHALL display a success indicator and the strategy tip for that question
6. WHILE in Full_Test_Mode, THE App SHALL NOT reveal answer correctness until the entire section is completed
7. WHILE in Full_Test_Mode, THE App SHALL allow the Student to navigate between questions using forward and backward controls and a question number grid showing answered and unanswered questions

### Requirement 10: Data Persistence and Performance

**User Story:** As a Student, I want my progress saved reliably, so that I can resume my studies across sessions and devices.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Student submits an answer, THE App SHALL persist the Performance_Record to the database within 3 seconds of submission
2. WHEN a Student logs in from a different device, THE App SHALL synchronize all Performance_Records, Weakness_Profile, and Study_Plan data within 10 seconds
3. IF a network interruption occurs during a session, THEN THE App SHALL cache responses locally and synchronize within 30 seconds of connectivity being restored, preserving the most recent response per question in the event of a conflict between local and server data
4. IF synchronization after connectivity restoration fails, THEN THE App SHALL retry synchronization up to 3 times at 10-second intervals and display an indicator to the Student that data is pending synchronization
5. WHILE at least 1000 Students are concurrently active, THE App SHALL return question retrieval responses within 3 seconds
